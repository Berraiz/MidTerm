Miss From Brazil

  1. "altinha" its a brazilian soccer game, its based on not letting the ball touch the ground, you usually use a lot of head, shoulder, feet and chest to play this game, its really fun
  2. The second thing i miss the most, would be "Feijoada", a tradicional Brazilian Dish based on beans and pig meat

Canada is Amazing
